## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and supportive.
- Assume good intent; ask questions to clarify.
- Keep discussions on-topic and about the work.
- Be open to feedback and different perspectives.
- Respect privacy and boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, insults, or persistent trolling.
- Sharing private info without consent (doxing).
- Sexualized language or unwelcome advances.
- Hijacking threads or repeatedly derailing conversations.
